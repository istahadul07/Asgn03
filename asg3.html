Cox's Bazar Sea Beach

Cox'Sea beach is a tourist city located in south-eastern Bangladesh alongside Bay of Bengal.It's Belongs to Cox's Bazar district.Cox's Bazar is famous for it's beauty.Here located the largest continuous natural sandy sea beach in the world,which is 120km long from cox's Bazar to Bodormokam.The largest finisg marine port of Bangladesh and the submarine cable landing station are also developed in Cox's Bazar.
